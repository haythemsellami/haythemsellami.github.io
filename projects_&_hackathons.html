UpSideDai (code)

Winner of ETHDenver 2020

A highly leveraged contract for difference (CFD) built on Uniswap and Maker(DAI) protocols. This mechanism enables traders and speculators to bet on and hedge
against price fluctuations of DAI by buying leveraged long or short positions. The CFD construction enables high leverage (20x) while remaining capital efficient
and not requiring high margin requirements (100% collateralization). Positions are priced against the market observable DAI/USD price by using a combination
of the Maker oracle and Uniswap.

Contribution: mainly worked on implementing the smart contracts and writing tests

Skills: Solidity, TS/JS
